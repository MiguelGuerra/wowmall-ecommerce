About store

What our online WowMall is?

We’re a fashion/apparel/sports store for the whole family! We have all subjects for outdoor activities - from the most basic sporting goods to the last generation technological equipment. It also plays an important role that we have an online store. Now, no matter where you live – our products are always there, turning on the computer will be enough!

A high-quality goods with a reasonable price – in such a way you can describe our approach to the formation of commodity policy that we are implementing for recent years. We carefully select suppliers, sifting unreasonably expensive, and supporting those who share our views.

Consultants prompt the optimal solution for each client, based on its needs and the level of sports training. Customer’s interests have always been for us on the first place. For regular customers we have a system of privileges. In addition, participants of special programs regularly receive special offers and discounts on services.

For whom we are?

For somebody, the sport is a serious climb to the peak; for someone is an easy walk by the bike. Both these descriptions are examples of active lifestyle, and we consider it in our assortment policy. We support children's sports hobbies, school programs, developing and traditional sports. Each of our customer is an athlete, every purchase is a step to a healthier nation.

At the same time, we pay great attention to the technological level of offered products, whether it be a children’s sportswear or shoes for professional trekking. We know that modern technology can significantly improve the efficiency and safety of sports activities, and you get maximum pleasure from the movement. And don’t accept half-measures, neither we nor our customers!

To choose the best sports equipment is rather difficult. But to make this decision is much easier when in front of you - really high-quality, carefully selected items. Online Store Woomall offers the best in world’s sport. Let’s agree: it is impossible to refuse such an offer!
